# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: November 2025**

---

## Data Collection

BuyThat does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All shopping data, including product information, store details, prices, and shopping lists, is stored locally on your device only.

## Local Storage

The following information is stored locally on your device:

- Product names, brands, and tags
- Store names and locations
- Price information and history
- Shopping lists and items
- Measurement units and conversions
- App preferences

*This data remains on your device and is never shared with third parties or transmitted over the internet.*

## No Third-Party Services

BuyThat does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. Your shopping data remains completely private.

## Data Deletion

You can delete your shopping data at any time by:

- Deleting products, stores, shopping lists, or price history individually within the app
- Uninstalling the app (removes all local data)

## Children's Privacy

BuyThat does not knowingly collect any data from children or adults. The app is designed to work entirely offline with local storage only.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app and on our support page.
